Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to have a space where potential customers can come to learn more about white water rafting and book appointments if they are interested.

Audience

My audience will be families who want to go white water rafting on vacation. The person likely to be on the website are a parent, age 40-50 (likely their children will be old enough to handle white water rafting at that point.) They are most likely well off financially if they can afford to pay for a family white water rafting vacation. My audience will probably access my website via laptop/desktop.
